About Our Platform

Advancing Traffic Surveillance

Our platform leverages video and image processing for traffic surveillance, analysis, and monitoring in urban areas. Using a stationary camera mounted on freeways, we estimate vehicle speeds with an average error of ±7 km/h. By combining classic computer vision techniques with modern deep learning models, including optical flow and multi-scale convolutional networks, we provide accurate and efficient speed detection.

Designed for scalability, our system supports applications like traffic flow prediction, anomaly detection, vehicle re-identification, and tracking. These capabilities help prevent traffic collisions and improve road planning by better estimating transit demand, making it a vital tool for smart cities and safer roads.
